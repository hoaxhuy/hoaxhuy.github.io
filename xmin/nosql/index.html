Data Fundamentals

This material provides an overview of data fundamentals. It explains the definition of data and its various types, including structured, unstructured, and semi-structured data. The video also discusses common file formats for transferring data between systems and introduces relational and non-relational databases as data storage solutions. Relational databases are used for day-to-day business activities, while non-relational databases offer flexibility in handling diverse and unstructured data. Overall, the material emphasizes the importance of efficient data management and storage for effective analysis and decision-making.
